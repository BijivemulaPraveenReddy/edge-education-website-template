Full Academic Year

EDGE education & services provides, through the medium of English, a curriculum for a two – year course of study - for Pre-University Courses in State of Karnataka.

Batches formed around Feb-April

The course for study in Science and Commerce is so designed that the students, at the end of PUC 2nd year (Std.12) are in a state of preparedness to attempt various competitive examinations, particularly in Engineering, Medicine.

Long term (Full Academic Year) Batches formed around Feb-April

1 YEAR PROGRAM

In this program, student is offered coaching for II PUC and their respect entrance exams. This course is for students opting at 2nd PUC (std 12). Students are prepared for Board exam + Entrance Exams. The student will not be taught 2 years of syllabus from scratch, but to optimize students’ performance we offer crash courses at the beginning and around end of the academic year. Weekly model tests/Mock Exams are given to students. The tests are as per the latest Board Examination centric pattern and evaluations. We provide an environment that simulates the official examination conditions that helps every student to be prepared to use their time optimally and efficiently without stressing out.

2 YEAR PROGRAM

In this program the student is offered coaching for I & II PUC and their respective entrance exams. Students from 1st PUC (Std 11th) will able to opt-in for this program. Experienced senior faculty will guide the students through the chapters and portions that contain maximum weightage in board exams. Weekly tests will be conducted to track the progress of the student.
