About EDGE

EDGE

WHO WE ARE- More than just a tutorial center, We help students realize their true potential, with experienced guidance, curated content and knowledge beyond books in an interactive environment. We run expertise in examination training for PUC, CET & NEET, and also offer foreign language courses and more.

VISION- “Education Made Easy” We aim to onboard students seeking help academically to help guide and certify their trust in the courses they choose to pursue. With our support from certified tutors, study material is curated to fit each course as well as cover its textbook course within.

MISSION- “Empowering young minds achieve excellence in a holistic environment” Our course material reflects effort strewn from each of the teachers driving the subject course. We believe in transparency, and our content talks for the same. Your children will be encouraged to speak up and engage in class as it helps build confidence in learning the subject and otherwise.
